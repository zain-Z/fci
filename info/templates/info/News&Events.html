Computer and Information Conference of Suez Canal University recommends activating technology




The first international scientific conference entitled "Advanced Computer Communications and Informatics", which was held on Saturday and Sunday at the Faculty of Computers and Information, Suez Canal University, under the patronage of Dr. Mamdouh Ghorab, President of the University, recommended activating the role of information technology in developing the economic zone in the Suez Canal axis



The conference, over the course of two days, honored a number of research participants, experts and university professors, and the opening of the conference dealt with several speeches by the conference organizers, and Dr. Abdel Raouf Abdel-Rahman Mustafa, Dean of the Faculty of Computers and Information, stated that the college's first international scientific conference represents a unique move in the field of computers and information technology
